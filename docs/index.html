Four Chapters

Real brands. Real dilemmas.

Chapter 3

How It Works

Play. Decide. Compare.

The game takes approximately 20–30 minutes to complete.

1
Enter your name & email

Your results are saved automatically so the class can compare strategies after the session.

2
Make two decisions per chapter

Each choice moves your Investor Confidence and ESG Credibility scores up or down.

3
See what actually happened

After each chapter, your decisions are contrasted with the real corporate outcome — revealed at the end of each round.

4
Check the class ranking

A live leaderboard ranks all players by their average score — visible to the whole class.

Scoring

Two metrics. One tension.

Your ranking is determined by the average of both scores — rewarding balanced governance, not just profit maximisation.

📊
Investor Confidence

Reflects how financial markets and shareholders perceive your strategic decisions. Starts at 10.

🌿
ESG Credibility

Reflects how stakeholders, NGOs, and the public assess your environmental and social commitments. Starts at 10.
